BOOTH INFORMATION

The booths are inside Dewan Tun Canselor.

1. Digital Ticket Booth

For students who have completed their student account's registration, head to the Digital Ticket Booth in order to get a physical print of your ticket. The ticket includes your student information and is needed in order to get all the necessities during the day.

2. Goodies Booth

After getting the physical print of your ticket, head over to the goodies booth to get MMU/WIN t-shirt along with MMU tote bag.

3. Badge Booth

Head over to the Badge Booth in order to get your student-ID.
